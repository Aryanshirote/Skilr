🚀 About Skilr Resume Analyzer

🎯 Our Mission

Skilr helps students and job seekers understand their resumes using intelligent analysis, real-time scoring, and personalized improvement suggestions.

⚙️ What We Do

  • Resume Skill Extraction using NLP
  • Resume Score Analysis (job-based scoring)
  • Skill Gap Identification
  • Job Role Matching (Frontend, Backend, etc.)
  • Skill Visualization with charts

🧠 How It Works

Upload your resume → Skilr extracts your skills → Matches them with industry job roles → Calculates your score → Suggests missing skills → Displays insights using dashboards.

💻 Technology Used

  • Django (Backend Framework)
  • Python (Core Logic & Analysis)
  • Matplotlib (Skill Charts)
  • HTML, CSS (Frontend UI)
  • SQLite / Database Integration

🔥 Why Skilr is Powerful?

Unlike traditional resume tools, Skilr doesn’t just store resumes — it analyzes them deeply, identifies skill gaps, and guides users toward the right job roles with data-driven insights.
